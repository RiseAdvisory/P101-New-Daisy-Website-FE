# Ethan Cole

**Headline:** Behavioral Data Analyst & Digital Conversion Strategist  
**Location:** New York, USA

## About
I work at the intersection of behavioral science, data analysis, and digital conversion strategy.

My academic background combines applied mathematics and data science. I earned a Bachelor of Science in Applied Mathematics from Columbia University, with a focus on probability theory, statistical modeling, and optimization, followed by a master's degree in Data Science from NYU with a specialization in behavioral analytics and predictive modeling.

My career began in tech and SaaS analytics, where I focused on e-commerce optimization, funnel analysis, user drop-off, and behavioral segmentation. Over time, I became increasingly interested in high-uncertainty categories—especially beauty and personal services—where users are not simply choosing products, but making emotionally loaded decisions under risk.

That shift led me into the beauty industry, where I have worked with skincare brands, booking platforms, marketplaces, and premium service businesses. My work centers on a simple idea: conversion does not usually fail because users lack options; it fails because the cost of deciding becomes too high.

I help teams reduce cognitive load, improve trust signals, structure choice more effectively, and turn interest into confident action.
