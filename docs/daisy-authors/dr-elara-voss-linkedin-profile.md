# Dr. Elara Voss

**Headline:** Beauty Business Strategist & Growth Architect  
**Location:** Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

## About
I help salon owners, clinic operators, and beauty entrepreneurs build businesses that are not just busy, but profitable, resilient, and designed to scale.

My background is in financial economics, entrepreneurial growth, and management consulting. I earned an MBA from INSEAD, after completing a bachelor's degree in Financial Economics at the American University of Beirut. Early in my career, I worked with SMEs across retail, hospitality, and service businesses, where I developed a reputation for diagnosing a common problem: growth that looked impressive on the surface but was weak underneath.

That perspective led me into the beauty industry. While consulting for a salon in Riyadh, I saw firsthand how talented founders could deliver exceptional services yet still struggle with margins, pricing, forecasting, and long-term growth planning. What looked like a salon problem was actually a business architecture problem.

Today, I specialize in helping beauty businesses improve unit economics, pricing, revenue modeling, capacity planning, and scalable systems. My work is grounded in a simple principle: profitable growth is designed, not discovered.
