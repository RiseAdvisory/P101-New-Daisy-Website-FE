# Sofia Alvarez

**Headline:** Salon Operations Consultant & Beauty Technology Strategist  
**Location:** Barcelona, Spain

## About
I help salons, spas, and beauty businesses build operational systems that reduce chaos, improve team performance, and make growth sustainable.

My background is in hospitality operations and business process management. I earned a bachelor's degree in Hospitality Management from Les Roches International School of Hotel Management in Switzerland, followed by a diploma in Business Process Management from IE Business School in Madrid.

Before specializing in beauty, I worked in hotel operations across Madrid and Lisbon, managing complex service environments where scheduling, staffing, process design, and service quality had to work together under pressure. That operational discipline became the foundation of my consulting work.

My entry into the beauty industry started with what looked like a simple scheduling problem in a Barcelona salon. In reality, it was a systems problem: disconnected software, unclear processes, weak commission logic, and operational habits that made day-to-day work harder than it needed to be. Solving that challenge led me to focus exclusively on beauty businesses.

Today, I advise salon owners and multi-location operators on booking architecture, staff operations, technology selection, payment workflows, process documentation, and implementation planning. My goal is simple: build systems that make the right thing the easy thing.
