# Amara Nasser

**Headline:** Independent Beauty Professional & Freelance Career Mentor  
**Location:** London, UK

## About
I help beauty professionals build sustainable freelance careers with the structure, financial clarity, and systems most of us were never taught when we entered the industry.

I trained in hairdressing at the London College of Fashion, earning an NVQ Level 3 in Hairdressing, and later completed a Business Management Certificate through the Open University. My career began in established London salons, where I developed strong technical foundations while also seeing the limits of traditional salon employment: unpredictable scheduling, weak compensation structures, and very little support for long-term independence.

At 26, I left salon employment and went freelance. That transition taught me a difficult but valuable lesson: being excellent at the craft is only part of the job. The rest is business setup, pricing, taxes, banking, insurance, client acquisition, boundaries, and learning how to create stability for yourself.

Over time, I built the systems that made independent work sustainable—first for myself, then for other beauty professionals asking how to do the same. Today, I combine active freelance practice with mentoring, writing, and workshops for stylists and other beauty professionals who want to go independent without learning every lesson the hard way.

I believe freedom in freelance comes from structure, not from winging it.
