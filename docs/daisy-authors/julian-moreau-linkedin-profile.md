# Julian Moreau

**Headline:** Beauty Industry Growth Strategist & Digital Marketing Specialist  
**Location:** Dubai, UAE

## About
I help beauty businesses turn visibility into bookings, and bookings into long-term client loyalty.

My background is in marketing, communications, and hands-on digital growth. I studied Marketing & Communications at Zayed University in Dubai and later expanded my technical toolkit through certifications in Google Digital Marketing, Meta Blueprint, HubSpot, Mailchimp, and SEMrush.

I began my career in a Dubai digital agency managing social media and digital campaigns across hospitality, retail, and service brands. That work gave me a strong foundation in channel strategy, content, paid media, analytics, and reporting, but I found my real focus when I started working with salons and beauty businesses.

Beauty marketing is different from generic digital marketing. It is visual, personal, trust-driven, and deeply connected to the booking experience. That is where I do my best work: helping salons, clinics, and independent professionals become easier to discover, easier to trust, and easier to book.

My approach connects the full growth journey: discovery, trust, conversion, retention, and advocacy. I care about practical execution, measurable results, and channel decisions that lead to real appointments—not vanity metrics.
